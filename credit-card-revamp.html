Context & Strategic Shift

The objective wasn't merely a facelift. We faced a critical technical pivot-migrating the product from a legacy system to a new Nuxt 3 micro-frontend setup. Simultaneously, the business demanded a shift toward a user focused & AI-assisted discovery to combat rising customer acquisition costs.

Evolving toward guided experiences for retention growth

Focusing on guided journeys and AI-supported choices to encourage repeat use and retention

Nuxt 3 Migration
